      What's New

      • Activities Public/Private Feature: Activities can now be set to Priv
        Activities Public/Private Feature: Activities can now be set to Private or Public. Activities will default to Private and can only be viewed by the creator or owner of the activity. All activities you own or were created by you will show up in a new section called My Activities. Private Activities will be designated with a lock icon.
        Read More>>
      • Rules for Private and Public Activities: Private Phone Call activities
        Rules for Private and Public Activities: Private Phone Call activities will not generate email reminders, nor will they trigger the creation of a future open Phone Call activity. Private activities can be updated to Public. All Public activities will be visible in the JLL Activities list on Companies, Contacts, Properties or Leases. Private Activities will not appear on the Rap Sheet emails or on the Broker Portal Rap Sheet.
        Read More>>
